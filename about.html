About Talidaga

Storytelling Through Systems, Design, and Creative Exploration
Creative Philosophy

Creativity has always been how I explore and understand the world. Over the years, that curiosity has led me through illustration, graphic design, photography, print production, content creation, tabletop game design, and game systems analysis.

While these disciplines may appear different on the surface, they are connected by the same goal: understanding how stories, systems, and visual communication shape the experiences people have.

Whether I am creating a creature design, building a visual identity, analyzing a gameplay system, documenting player experience, or developing marketing materials, I enjoy understanding how individual pieces contribute to a larger experience.

How My Work Connects

Illustration & Worldbuilding

Visual storytelling through creatures, environments, concept development, narrative illustration, and world-focused design.

Game Systems / UX / QA

Gameplay analysis, first-play observation, UX evaluation, structured testing, and system communication through a player-focused lens.

Graphic Design & Media

Branding, typography, marketing assets, content creation, and visual communication through Tal'Elymor Design.
